# Floating Solar Platform Maintenance SOP
**Document No:** FPS-OM-001  
**Effective Date:** 01 Jan 2024  
**Applicable To:** Maithon Dam 234 MWp Floating Solar Plant

## 1. Purpose
This document provides standard operating procedures for routine inspection and maintenance of the floating structures supporting PV modules.

## 2. Scope
Covers floats, walkways, mooring lines, anchor systems, and interconnection bolts.

## 3. Monthly Inspection Checklist
- Walk entire platform perimeter; check for visible deformation of HDPE floats.
- Verify float buoyancy – mark any float with >5% submersion.
- Inspect all M16 stainless steel fasteners – torque to 25 Nm.
- Check anti-slip surface of walkways for wear.
- Ensure bird deterrent spikes are intact.

## 4. Mooring System – Quarterly Inspection
- Measure tension on all mooring lines. Acceptable range: 8–12 kN.
- Inspect anchor shackles for corrosion; replace if pitting > 1mm.
- Check chain links for elongation; replace any link with >3% stretch.
- Verify position of floating island using GPS; deviation from design coordinates >2m requires re-tensioning.
- Record water level of reservoir and compare with line length; adjust for monsoon drawdown.

## 5. Monsoon Contingency
- Before monsoon onset (May/June), double-check all anchor line connections.
- Install additional temporary storm anchors if forecast wind >60 km/h.
- Clear drainage holes on all walkway floats to prevent waterlogging.

## 6. Safety Requirements
- Life jacket mandatory at all times on water.
- Hot work permit required for any welding on metal parts.
- Minimum 2-person team for platform inspection.
